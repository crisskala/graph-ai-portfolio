About the course

An 8-week live cohort course focused on practical, no-code GenAI workflows.

“Generative AI for Work & Research” is an 8-week live cohort course built around no-code tools for task automation and research productivity.

The program combines weekly live workshops, help sessions, community support, and a capstone project with coaching and feedback.

Module 1. Websites, Documents & PresentationsLLM basics; Markdown/LaTeX/HTML outputs; simple research websites; AI presentation tools.
Module 2. AI-Automated Data AnalysisFile transforms, dashboards, automated reports, quick analysis workflows.
Module 3. GenAI for Research & Text AnalysisLiterature search, text classification, summarization, and knowledge mapping.
Module 4. Custom GPTs & ChatbotsContext windows, RAG, custom assistants, chatbots with knowledge bases.
Module 5. AI for Task AutomationAgents, automation flows, monitoring, and browser-control workflows.
Module 6. GenAI for Communication & OutreachResearch dissemination, visuals/figures, video abstracts, audience-adapted summaries.
Module 7. Capstone Project & ShowcaseIterate with feedback, finalize a solution/tool, and present it publicly.
AssessmentQuizzes, workshop assignments, participation, and a final project.

About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course focused on no-code AI tools for task automation, research workflows, and practical outputs.

Bootcamp features

Hands-on and guided

  • Weekly live workshops and help sessions
  • Private community forums
  • Personalized feedback on assessments
  • Capstone project coaching and showcase
  • Shareable certificate and lifetime tutorial access
Grading

Clear expectations

  • Online quizzes: 15%
  • Workshop assignments: 35%
  • Attendance & participation: 10%
  • Final project: 40%
  • Passing grade: 80%
Outcome

Workflows you can reuse

The goal is to leave with repeatable systems: structured prompting, toolchains, templates, and automation patterns that translate into everyday work and research productivity.

Course modules

High-level overview of the modules described in the course brochure.

Module 1Websites, docs & presentations

Websites, documents & presentations

LLM fundamentals, structured outputs (Markdown, LaTeX, HTML), and building simple research websites with Canvas.

Module 2AI-automated analysis

AI-automated data analysis

Use AI tools to analyze data, transform files, build dashboards, and generate automated reports.

Module 3Research workflows

GenAI for research & text analysis

Literature workflows, classification, summarization, and synthesizing large document collections.

Module 4Assistants

Custom GPTs and chatbots

Context windows, RAG, and building custom assistants and chatbots for knowledge work.

Module 5Automation

AI for task automation

Agent-based flows, email automations, browser control agents, and orchestration patterns.

Module 6Communication

GenAI for communication & outreach

Summaries for different audiences, visuals, diagrams, video abstracts and dissemination workflows.

Module 7Capstone

Capstone project & showcase

Iterate on a final solution with peer feedback, coaching, troubleshooting, and a public showcase.
